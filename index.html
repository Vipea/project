This interactive visualization shows the influence of fishing methods on life in the North Sea.
Fishing methods such as trawling scraped nets over the sea floor, turning over the soil.
Many benthic animals have died as a result, which used to be the food source of small fish.
Bigger fish eat smaller fish, birds eat big fish... A perfect recipe for ecosystem imbalance.
Click in the legend for more information about the fishing method.
